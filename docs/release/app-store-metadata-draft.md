## Full description
Ring Knot is a calm, tactile untangling puzzle about a tray of interlocked metal
rings.

Every silver ring is an open band with a single gap. Drag around a ring to roll it
until its opening lines up with the way out, then pull it free. Small metal clips
and full closed anchor rings hold the others in place — clear the blockers in the
right order, then free the copper knot at the centre.

• Rotate open rings to find each gap
• Align the gap with the exit, then pull the ring out
• Clear the blocker clips that pin rings together
• Work around fixed full anchor rings that never move
• Free the copper knot to finish each level
• 20 hand-built levels with a gentle difficulty curve
• Procedural metal art with bridge bands and woven, interlocked tubes
• Soft haptics and sound you can toggle off

Plays entirely offline. No ads, no tracking, no accounts, no in-app purchases — just
the puzzle.

Designed for one-handed play, with Reduce Motion support, VoiceOver labels, and
Dynamic Type.
