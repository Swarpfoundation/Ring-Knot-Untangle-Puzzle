## Description

> Ring Knot is a calm, tactile untangling puzzle. Every piece is an open metal
> ring with a single gap — roll it under your finger until the opening faces the
> way out, then pull it free through the gap.
>
> But the rings are threaded through one another. A ring won't budge until the
> pieces crossing it are gone, so each board is a small knot you patiently take
> apart, one ring at a time, until the copper core slips loose.
>
> • Rotate-then-pull controls that feel like handling a real ring
> • 20 handcrafted levels that build from a single hook to a tangled corkscrew
> • A gentle on-ramp: forgiving early, precise later
> • Hints when you're stuck, and a quick restart whenever you want it
> • Designed for one hand, in portrait
>
> Built to respect you: no ads, no analytics, no accounts, no in-app purchases,
> and nothing sent off your device. Just you and the knot.
>
> Full VoiceOver support, Dynamic Type, and Reduce Motion.
